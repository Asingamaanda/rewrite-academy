### Types and Methods

### Method (Linear + Quadratic)

3. Solve the resulting equation
4. Find the other variable
5. **VERIFY** both solutions in both original equations

## ðŸŽ“ Exam Tips

1. **Read the question carefully**
   - How many decimal places?
   - What form should the answer be in?

2. **Show ALL working**
   - Marks are for method, not just answer
   - Even if you get the wrong answer, you can get method marks

3. **Check your answers**
   - Substitute back into original equation
   - Does it make sense?

4. **Manage your time**
   - Don't spend too long on one question
   - Move on and come back if stuck

5. **Common errors to avoid**
   - Forgetting to check surd equations
   - Accepting negative values for exponentials
   - Dividing by variable without considering it could be 0
   - Wrong interval notation for inequalities
